Hi, I am Kelstar.

My journey began in IT, where I worked as a developer and learned to think with structure, clarity, and precision.

After moving to Australia, I entered the world of health and care through nursing and massage therapy. Over time, I became deeply drawn to the human body — how it moves, how it ages, how it holds stress, and how it can recover through thoughtful care.

Today, I work as a massage therapist in Sydney, combining practical bodywork with curiosity, observation, and a genuine care for people.

I love coffee, fashion, greens, flowers, and books. In my free time, I enjoy gym workouts and writing about wellbeing, body care, ageing, and everyday recovery.
