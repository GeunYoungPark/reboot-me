Unbreakable

A Woman’s Guide to Aging with Power by Dr. Vonda Wright

This book is a health guide that reinterprets women’s aging and overall health through a scientific lens focused on muscle, hormones, and inflammation.

Dr. Vonda Wright explains that changes in women’s bodies during midlife are not simply a natural decline of aging, but are closely linked to loss of muscle mass, hormonal shifts, and chronic inflammation.

The book highlights an important message: recovery does not come from exercise alone. A healthier body requires a wider reset — food, sleep, stress control, movement, and daily lifestyle habits all work together.

Recovery doesn’t come from exercise alone — it requires diet, sleep, stress control, and a full lifestyle reset.

What I found meaningful is that this book does not treat aging as something women must simply accept. Instead, it encourages women to understand their bodies more clearly and make practical changes that support strength, energy, and long-term wellbeing.

For anyone interested in women’s health, menopause, inflammation, muscle loss, and healthy aging, this book offers a strong and empowering starting point.
